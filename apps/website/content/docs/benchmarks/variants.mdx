## Summary

The benchmark results provide a clear comparison between the `cvb` and `cva` packages, highlighting their respective strengths and use cases:

- **Recipe Creation**: The `cva` package exhibits a slight performance advantage in recipe creation. It is marginally faster across various scenarios, including simple, complex, and large recipes.
- **Recipe Usage**: The `cvb` package excels in recipe usage, significantly outperforming `cva`. It demonstrates remarkable efficiency, particularly in complex and large recipes, achieving up to 14x the performance of `cva`. This positions `cvb` as the ideal choice for applications that prioritize frequent and high-performance recipe usage.

In summary, while `cva` is slightly better for initial recipe creation, `cvb` offers unparalleled performance in real-world usage scenarios, making it the superior option for most applications.

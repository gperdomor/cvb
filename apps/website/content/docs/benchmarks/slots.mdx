## Summary

The benchmark results clearly demonstrate the superior performance of the Class Variance Builder compared to Tailwind Variants in both recipe creation and usage.

- **Recipe Creation**: The `cvb` package consistently outperforms `tailwind-variants` in creating slot recipes for multipart components. Across all scenariosâ€”simple, complex, and large recipesâ€”`cvb` achieves significantly higher operations per second, making it the faster and more efficient choice for recipe creation.
- **Recipe Usage**: When it comes to resolving styles, `cvb` again outshines `tailwind-variants`. It delivers faster performance in all tested scenarios, including simple and complex recipes. The performance gap widens further in large recipes, where `cvb` demonstrates remarkable efficiency.

In summary, `cvb` is the clear winner in both recipe creation and usage, offering unparalleled speed and efficiency compared to `tailwind-variants`. This makes `cvb` the ideal choice for developers seeking high-performance solutions for managing styles in multipart components.
